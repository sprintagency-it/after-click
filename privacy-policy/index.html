Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how AfterClick collects and uses personal data submitted through this website and related request forms.

Controller

AfterClick is operated by Lorenzo Mottola. Contact: support@after-click.com. Public VAT ID: IT16566571002.

Data we may collect

  • Contact details such as name, email and billing details.
  • Store and business context submitted for a Revenue Map request.
  • Public URLs, product pages, ad angles or screenshots voluntarily provided.
  • Technical data such as page visits, consent preferences and device/browser information.
  • Payment and invoice data collected through Stripe.

What not to submit

AfterClick does not need passwords, admin access, private customer lists, sensitive personal data, private dashboards or private financial records for the first Revenue Map.

Purposes

  • Responding to requests and processing paid orders.
  • Preparing and delivering the AfterClick Revenue Map.
  • Managing payments, invoices and customer communication.
  • Improving the website and measuring marketing performance where consent is given.
  • Complying with legal, tax and accounting obligations.

Service providers

Depending on the final live setup, data may be processed through Cloudflare for hosting and functions, Stripe for payment and billing records, Tally for intake forms, Google Sheets for operational tracking, Resend for transactional email, Meta for consent-based measurement, and operational tools used to prepare the report.

Legal bases

Processing may be based on pre-contractual steps, contract performance, legal obligations, legitimate interests for basic business operations, and consent for non-essential measurement or advertising cookies.

Retention

Order and delivery data is kept only as long as needed for order routing, delivery, support, accounting and reasonable business records. Payment and invoice data may be retained according to applicable legal and tax requirements.

Your rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, limitation, portability or objection where applicable. You may also withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact support@after-click.com.
