Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how AfterClick uses cookies and similar technologies on this website.

What cookies are

Cookies and similar technologies are small pieces of data stored by your browser or device. They can help a website work, remember preferences or measure usage.

Categories

Category Purpose Default
Necessary Required for basic page behavior and remembering your cookie choice. Always active
Measurement and advertising May help understand which pages, ads or messages bring relevant ecommerce teams to AfterClick. Only after consent

Current setup

This site includes a lightweight consent banner. Meta Pixel measurement is configured for page views and key funnel events such as offer views, checkout starts, purchases and intake confirmations, but it is only loaded after measurement and advertising consent.

How to manage preferences

You can accept, reject or customize non-essential cookies through the banner. You can also reopen preferences from the footer link.

Changes

If new analytics, advertising, payment, video, form or CRM tools are added, this page should be updated before public traffic.
