The point of view

Paid traffic does not create clarity, trust or margin by itself.

It exposes whether the journey after the click is ready. If your ad promise, product page, offer, proof, cart and follow-up are disconnected, increasing spend only makes the weak spots louder.

99% -> 1% bridge

The gap is not one page. It is the system.

Most ecommerce stores operate like separate parts: ads, product page, offer, proof, cart, checkout and follow-up all pulling in different directions.

Top ecommerce brands connect those parts into one conversion system. AfterClick shows which parts of that system are missing, weak or disconnected in your store.

FAQ

What buyers usually want to know.

Why does AfterClick exist?

Most ecommerce stores do not need another pile of opinions. They need to see the gap between scattered after-click pieces and the more connected systems used by mature ecommerce brands. AfterClick maps that gap into priorities your team can act on.

What do I actually receive?

You receive a private HTML report link, a shareable PDF export and an AI-ready Markdown file. The map includes a Revenue Leak Score, the 5 biggest leaks, first actions, a 7-day fix roadmap and test ideas tied to what the diagnosis found.

How detailed is the Revenue Map?

It reviews message match, PDP, buy box, proof, cart and checkout friction, lifecycle recovery, creative testing opportunities and tracking or profit clarity signals. The final report stays focused on the highest-priority leaks so it remains usable.

How is this different from a generic CRO audit or AI scan?

AfterClick is paid-traffic aware and built around the after-click system, not a generic checklist. The process is AI-assisted, but the final priorities are human-reviewed and organized around decisions that can guide a founder, team or AI agent.

Is this only for Shopify?

No. AfterClick is for ecommerce stores. Shopify stores are the best fit for the first version because many conversion, cart, checkout and lifecycle patterns are easier to review and act on.

Do you need access to my store?

No private access is required for the standard Revenue Map. We review public pages, your intake answers and any ad, page or email screenshots or links you choose to share.

Will you guarantee a conversion lift?

No. The report identifies likely leaks and prioritizes fixes. It does not guarantee revenue, ROAS, conversion rate or profit outcomes.

Is this a redesign or heatmap report?

No. The first Revenue Map is a diagnostic and fix-priority report. It does not include a full Figma redesign, eye-tracking heatmaps or user testing.

Can you implement the fixes?

If the report shows clear opportunities, we can scope a separate AfterClick Fix Sprint around the highest-priority fixes.
